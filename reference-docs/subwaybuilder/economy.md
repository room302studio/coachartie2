# Economy

## Important: 365x Multiplier

Each game day = 1 financial year. All revenue and costs multiplied by 365.

## Revenue

- Fare per passenger: $3.00 default (adjustable $0.25-$10.00)
- Revenue = passengers × fare × 365

## Starting Money

- Easy Mode: $3 billion
- Sandbox: Unlimited

## Track Costs (per meter)

Base cost:
- Heavy Metro: $50,000/m
- Light Metro: $30,000/m

### Elevation Multipliers

| Depth/Height | Multiplier |
|--------------|------------|
| Deep bore (< -100m) | 4.5x |
| Standard tunnel (-24 to -100m) | 2.0x |
| Cut & cover (-10 to -24m) | 1.0x |
| At grade (-3 to -10m) | 0.3x |
| Elevated (> 4.5m) | 0.8x |

### Water Crossing Multipliers (additional)

| Type | Multiplier |
|------|------------|
| Deep bore | 1.44x |
| Standard tunnel | 1.5x |
| Cut & cover | 3.0x |
| At grade | 10.0x |
| Elevated | 2.5x |

### Width Multipliers

| Tracks | Multiplier |
|--------|------------|
| Single | 0.75x |
| Dual | 1.0x |
| Quad | 1.5x |

## Station Costs

- Heavy Metro: $75M base
- Light Metro: $50M base
- Same elevation/water/width multipliers apply

## Operating Costs

Charged every 15 game minutes:

| Type | Base/hr | Per car/hr |
|------|---------|------------|
| Heavy Metro | $500 | $50 |
| Light Metro | $400 | $40 |

Example: 5-car heavy = $750/hr

## Bonds (Easy Mode Only)

| Size | Principal | Interest/day | Requires |
|------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Small | $100M | 6% | $10M/day revenue |
| Medium | $500M | 4% | $100M/day revenue |
| Large | $1B | 2% | $200M/day revenue |

One bond at a time. Must meet revenue requirement.
